Patient Life Challenge: Short Time, High Risk

Thousands of patients die every year not because hospitals are far — but because **medical help arrives late**. Urban traffic, blocked roads, remote locations, and slow ground ambulances create deadly delays during the “golden hour.”

  • Ambulances stuck in traffic for 30–60 minutes
  • Accidents in remote villages with no fast transport
  • Critical emergency where every minute matters
  • Delays reduce survival chance drastically

SkyFly solves this by reaching patients in **8–10 minutes**, flying directly above traffic

Concept Overview

SkyFly is a fully autonomous VTOL emergency air ambulance designed to overcome ground traffic, reach patients instantly, and transport them to hospitals with zero pilot involvement.

Autonomous Flight AI

Smart routing, auto takeoff/landing, emergency override.

Medical Emergency Cabin

Stretcher-ready cabin with essential emergency space.

Top-Mounted Rotor System

6 rods × 2 motors per rod (dual coaxial) = 12 motors for safety.

Designed for Urban & Rural

Flies over congested cities, villages, and disaster areas.

How SkyFly Works (Mobile App)

1. Emergency Request
Patient or bystander requests via mobile app.
2. Auto Activation
Nearest aircraft prepares automatically.
3. Autonomous Takeoff
Vertical lift — no pilot, no runway.
4. Fly Over Traffic
Direct path with no road delays.
5. Patient Loading
Side-access cabin for stretcher.
6. Hospital Delivery
Fastest route chosen automatically.
